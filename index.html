Let's play Black Jack!
The rules are simple! Get as close to 21 without going over! If you go over 21, you automatically lose. If you get 21 first thing, you win!
Number cards are worth their numerical value. Face cards all equal 10. Aces are 11!
To start out, you're going to get two cards. If your cards aren't over 21, you can take another card. If you STILL aren't over 21, you can take ANOTHER CARD!
Or you can decide to just stay with the hand you have. No need to be greedy, right?
The dealer then gets to do the same. At the end, winner is the closest to 21 without going over. Like stated at the beginning.
Highscore is your most consecutive hands without a loss!
Now go play cards!
